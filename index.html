How to use?

- Choose width/height then press "Submit" and enjoy!

- You can not choose values greater than 50.

Features

- To draw : hold Left-Click while moving your mouse over the cells.

- To erase : hold Right-Click while moving your mouse over the cells.

- To pick this color (that you are hovering on) : simply left-click the cell while holding SHIFT.

- You can use options buttons to add/remove Raws/Columns.

- Clear Canvas button removes all colors from cells to start again.

- Finished drawing ? Hit the "Done" button to see your drawing clearly !
